It could only be worse

You see, I’m reading that many new sites like Twitter want to launch. WHY? WHY DO THE SAME OVER AND OVER AGAIN? Bring something new to the table.

Uncensorable social media?

You see, with decentralized solutions like Mastodon (and other ActivityPub compatible software). We can achieve this. Every instance can freely block/whitelist other nodes as they please. Echo chamber will still pop-up: but you can more clearly navigate them.

Meta’s comeback?
