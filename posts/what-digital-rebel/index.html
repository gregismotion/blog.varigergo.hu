What does it mean to be a Digital Rebel?

digital rebellion,  digital rebel,  manifesto,  free software,  movement

2023.04.21 00:00

Gergő Vári

A digital rebel is part of the NEW generation of users: faster, more flexible and free.

A digital rebel is independent of big tech companies and can solve issues with the community.

A digital rebel needs no customer support: they can work together with other volunteers.

A digital rebel uses every information and tool that’s available for a good cause: even those which are forbidden. They share every piece of code, documentation and learning material with their fellows.

A digital rebel creates value and is ethical above all else. They’ll not bend to the wills of Big Tech.

If you ask so called “experts” in these fields: they will say this is impossible. Yet we are independent, and we function.

Digital rebels…

  • …make their own luck,
  • …are independent,
  • …think free,
  • …and change the world.

Viva la Digital Rebellion!
